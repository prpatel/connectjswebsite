CONNECT.TECH is the Southeast’s premier annual conference dedicated to JavaScript, Web and Mobile related technologies, tools, and frameworks. CONNECT.TECH will feature presentations and workshops from global technology and thought leaders as well as local community speakers. The 2017 event had 1100 people and we are planning for 1500 total people for 2018.
Ticket fee includes breakfast, lunch, snacks, drinks, after-parties, tshirts, entry into raffle prizes and more!
